Project Portfolio

The main project(s) that I am working on right now


Project Twitter Literature (TwitLit)

A Digital Humanities project that seeks to study, visualize, and preserve the global writing community developing on Twitter.


DH@Bucknell
Networking Initiative

Planned, developed, and launched a networking initiative to connect Digital Humanities students, scholars, and practitioners at and beyond Bucknell.

Projects that I have worked on in the past

UVA Reveal

An Augmented Reality (AR) app devoted to revealing otherwise hidden spaces and histories at the University of Virginia.

DH@UVA

A Digital Humanities initiative devoted to implementing the Graduate Certificate in Digital Humanities at the UVA

Circulating Spaces

A podcast series that explores what it means to engage with literature as a global-scale community.

Scholars' Lab Makerspace

As a Makerspace Technologist, I assisted students and professors with research projects and maintained the 3D printers.

Collective Biographies
of Women

I researched the lives of 19th century women and employed TEI to record the narrative events in the biographies of these women.

The Modernist Journal

I served as a Research Assistant for the Modernist Journals Project, using TEI and MODS to digitize early 20th century magazines.
